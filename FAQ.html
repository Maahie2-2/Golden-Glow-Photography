Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

I am located in Gaston, SC and can travel throughout the Columbia area at no extra cost up to 40 miles! I am able to travel to your ideal photo spot. We can discuss any travel fees required.

Do you require a deposit?

I do require a 50% non-refundable retainer and a signed contract to reserve your booking.

When will we get our photos?

You will receive a private online gallery. My turnaround time for portrait sessions is up to 5-10 business days depending on the type of session. For weddings, my turnaround time is up to 30 days. Rush orders are available.

What happens in case of bad weather?

During severe weather, I don’t want to be outside just as much as you. We can reschedule for a date that works for us both. If we cannot find a date right away, you may put your retainer towards any session of your choice.

How many pictures will we receive?

You will receive ALL of the enhanced image’s based on your package.

Do you do retouch or photoshop?

I edited images to retain their original essence. I do not generally do massive photoshop edits to change your intrinsic features.
